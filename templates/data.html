Why Pack?

Standard training processes one example at a time. If your context window is 4096 but your example is only 500 tokens, 88% of your compute is wasted on padding tokens.

Sample Packing concatenates multiple examples together, separated by an EOS token, to fill every slot in the sequence.

✓ Reduces training time by 2x to 10x.
