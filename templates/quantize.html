Why Quantize?

Large models like Llama-3 70B require 140GB of VRAM in FP16. By quantizing to 4-bit, we can fit that same model into 40GB, allowing it to run on consumer hardware with minimal loss in perplexity.

The "Quantization Tax"

Reducing bit-depth introduces "rounding errors." Below 4 bits, the model's ability to reason begins to degrade sharply as the weights can no longer represent subtle linguistic nuances.
